Tomato Soup

Description

As its name implies, this is a soup made almost entirely out of tomatoes. With some onions, garlic and pepper to add to the flavor, this tasty soup is perfect for any occasion, and very easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g tomatoes
  • half an onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • half a green pepper, diced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and boil the tomatoes for 1 min. After that, take them out and rinse them in cold water.
  2. Peel the tomatoes and dice them. Put them aside for later.
  3. Add the olive oil to a saucepan, and add the onion, green pepper and garlic. Cook them until the onion and garlic are golden brown.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, the bay leaf and some salt and pepper.
  5. Add 9 cups of water. If you prefer to, you can also use some vegetable broth.
  6. Set the heat to low and let boil for 30 min while stirring every once in a while.
  7. After the 30 min had passed you can either:
    • Leave the soup as it is, with the vegetable chunks, or
    • Blend the soup. If you choose to do so, remove the bay leaf first.
  8. After blending (or not), your soup will be ready. Enjoy!
