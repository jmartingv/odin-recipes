Chilaquiles with chicken

Description

For all you spicy food lovers, this traditional mexican dish can accompany you at any meal of the day, be it breakfast, lunch or dinner!

Ingredients

For the tortilla chips:

  • 1kg corn tortillas
  • 500 ml cooking oil

For the sauce:

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 serrano chiles
  • 1/4 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • half a cup of water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 chicken breast, boiled and shredded
  • 1 cup light sour cream
  • 200g shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Steps

For the tortilla chips

  1. Cut the tortillas into triangles (like nachos).
  2. Fry in cooking until golden brown.
  3. Drain on an absorbent paper towel and add salt.

For the sauce

  1. Boil the tomatoes and the chiles for 3 - 5 min.
  2. Blend them together with the onions, garlic and water until you get a light sauce.
  3. Stir fry in a saucepan with some olive oil.
  4. Set heat to medium and cook for 5 - 7 min.
  5. Add the tortilla chips to the mix, cook for 5 - 7 min.
