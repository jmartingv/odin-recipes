Spaghetti bolognese

Description

A well known, simple to cook, yet delicious recipe. Perfect if you're an italian cuisine enthusiast but are not very talented in the kitchen. This recipe can be easily prepared and can provide several servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • half an onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • half a tomato, diced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 200g spaghetti
  • half a cup of water
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • salt, pepper
  • serrano chile, diced (optional)

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan on medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.
  2. Add the garlic clove, onion, tomato and serrano chile to the saucepan, cook for 3 - 5 min.
  3. Add the ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook stirring for 5 min, or until it's browned over.
  4. After the beef's browned over, add both the tomato sauce and the half cup of water.
  5. Set the heat to medium-low, let boil for 15-20 min, be careful not to let it get too dry or burnt.
  6. While waiting for the sauce to boil, prepare the 400g spaghetti following the pack instructions.
  7. Drain the spaghetti and rinse it with running tap water.
  8. Serve the spaghetti on a bowl with the sauce on top, or mix them together in the saucepan. Enjoy!
